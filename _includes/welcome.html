Salve

On this web-site you will find dictionaries I made during my study of Latin language. List of the available wordbooks could be found in the particular section.

Dictionaries are encoded into XDXF format, and intended to be used on computer. This format is supported by desktop and mobile dictionary applications. See documentation for additional information.

I am open for cooperation. If you know how to use such software as unpaper or Scan Tailor, you could help improving scans. Of course, transcript of the dictionary articles would be much appreciated. If you prefer to work independently, I could add links to your Latin dictionaries into the collection.

In any case feel free to contact me or create an issue in github repository.
