Welcome

On this web-site you will find dictionaries I made during my study of Latin language.

Dictionaries are encoded into XDXF format, and intended to be used on computer. This format is supported by a dozen of dictionary shells, such a GoldenDict (Windows, Linux, MacOS) or Alpus (Android); see full list of compatible applications on Wikipedia.

The main sections of the site are list of dictionaries and recommendations for their use with GoldenDict.
